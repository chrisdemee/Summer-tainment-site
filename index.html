Lay Down My Life Tour

Summer Concert

OVERVIEW

During the summer, I went to a concert for the artist JPEGMAFIA. He's an innovative rapper and producer known for his genre-blurring sound that fuses elements of hip-hop, punk, and experimental music. He gained prominence with his raw, unfiltered lyrics and distinctive production style, often incorporating glitchy beats and unconventional samples.

REVIEW

This concert was the best part of my summer and the best one I've been to by far. I was highly anticipating this concert from the moment I got tickets. This was also my first experience in a moshpit so i was scared but it was 100% worth going. JPEGMAFIA did all of his hit songs and many songs from his latest album "I lay down my life for you". Overall, this was one of the best nights of my life that I wish I could do over again.
